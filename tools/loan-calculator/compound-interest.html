About Compound Interest

Compound interest is calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods. This results in exponential growth over time.

A = P(1 + r/n)nt

Where: A = Final amount, P = Principal, r = Annual interest rate, n = Compounding frequency, t = Time in years
