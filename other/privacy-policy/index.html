Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Introduction

Welcome to Shankar Group Tools ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe online experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our tools and services.

By using Shankar Group Tools, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

Important: Shankar Group Tools is a client-side application. All calculations and data processing occur locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you interact with our tools (features used, duration, errors encountered)
  • Device Information: Basic device information (browser type, screen resolution, operating system)
  • Local Storage: Data you choose to save locally on your device (calculator history, preferences)
  • Contact Information: Only if you voluntarily contact us via email

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Financial data or transaction information
  • Location data (GPS information)
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Photos or media files from your device

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To enhance existing tools and develop new features
  • Performance Analysis: To understand usage patterns and optimize performance
  • Bug Fixing: To identify and resolve technical issues
  • User Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure service integrity

Local Processing: All calculations, conversions, and data processing happen directly on your device. No sensitive data is transmitted to our servers.

Data Storage & Security

We prioritize the security of your information:

  • Local Storage: Your tool preferences and history are stored locally on your device using browser storage technologies
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your calculation history or personal data on cloud servers
  • Encryption: All communications with our servers (when necessary) use HTTPS encryption
  • Data Retention: Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 90 days for analysis purposes
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser cache

Your Control: Since data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You can clear your browser cache at any time to remove all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for specific functionality:

  • Font Awesome: For icons and visual elements (privacy policy available on fontawesome.com)
  • CDN Services: For loading external libraries and resources
  • Analytics: Basic anonymous analytics to understand usage patterns

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them for complete information.

No Advertising: Shankar Group Tools does not display advertisements and does not use advertising networks that track user behavior.

Children's Privacy

Shankar Group Tools is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information from our records.

Our tools are designed to be family-friendly and safe for educational use under parental supervision.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of data collection (though this may affect functionality)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Browser Settings: You can control many aspects of data collection through your browser settings, including cookie preferences and cache management.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Post a notice on our website for significant changes
  • Provide a summary of changes for major updates

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Continued Use: Your continued use of Shankar Group Tools after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: Click to Email Support

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours

We take privacy concerns seriously and will investigate any issues promptly.
