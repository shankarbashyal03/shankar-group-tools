Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the tools offline?

All Shankar Group Tools work offline once loaded. Just visit the website once while online, and you'll be able to use all tools without an internet connection. The website will automatically cache itself for offline use.

Are my calculations and data stored on servers?

No, all calculations and data processing happen locally on your device. We don't store any personal data, calculation history, or results on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices?

Yes! All tools are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. They work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size.

How often are new tools added?

We regularly update our tool collection based on user feedback and demand. New tools are added monthly. You can suggest new tools through our feedback form.

Is there a cost to use these tools?

All Shankar Group Tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. We believe in providing valuable tools without barriers.

How can I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs through our feedback form or by emailing support. Please include details about the issue, your device/browser, and steps to reproduce the problem for faster resolution.
